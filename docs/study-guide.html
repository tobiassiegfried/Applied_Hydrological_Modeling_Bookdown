Applied Modeling of Hydrological Systems in Central Asia

Study guide

This course book has been designed to accompany a 2-week workshop for aspiring water professionals at the Kazakh-German University in Almaty (Master level). The material can be covered in 8 intensive work days. The workshop participants will be guided through building up their own hydrological model of a sample catchment in Central Asia. The readers are recommended to work through the texts in each chapter including the tasks that serve to deepen reflection on the course material and to do their daily homework on the modeling of the sample catchment. This chapter explains how to use this course book.
